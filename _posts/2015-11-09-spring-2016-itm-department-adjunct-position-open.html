Hello all

The ITM department at IIT is looking for an adjunct to teach Data Warehousing. Here is a sample Objectives and Outcomes of the course and some previous books used. There is a syllabus available as well if you contact me at hajek@iit.edu Class will be taught Wednesday night 6:25 to 9:05 here in Chicago at the IIT main campus on state and 35th street. From Jan to April - 16 week semester.

Course Objective and Outcome
Course Objectives:
Each successful student will demonstrate foundation knowledge and application the following skills:

  • Clearly understand the top down (William Inmon) and bottom-up (Ralph Kimball) approaches for building data warehouses.</li>
  • Correctly use data warehousing and business intelligence terminology.</li>
  • Explain and apply the Business Dimensional Lifecycle</li>
  • Perform multidimensional analysis</li>
  • Describe data warehouse infrastructure issues.</li>
  • Determine the need for and management of meta data</li>
  • Explain the components of a data warehouse technical architecture.</li>
  • Describe techniques for data extraction transformation and loading into a data warehouse</li>
  • Explain the techniques used for data presentation by analytical applications</li>
  • Demonstrate the techniques for building a dimensional data mart/warehouse.</li>
    </ul>

    Course Outcomes:

    • Will possess an understanding of the top down (William Inmon) and bottom-up (Ralph Kimball)approaches for building data warehouses.</li>
    • Will possess an understanding of how to correctly use data warehousing and business intelligence terminology</li>
    • Will possess an understanding of to explain and apply the Business Dimensional Lifecycle</li>
    • Will possess an understanding of how to perform multidimensional analysis</li>
    • Will possess an understanding of how to accurately and efficiently describe data warehouse infrastructure issues</li>
    • Will possess an understanding of how to accurately and efficiently determine the need for and management of meta data</li>
    • Will possess an understanding of how to accurately and efficiently explain the components of a data warehouse technical architecture.</li>
    • Will possess an understanding of how to accurately and efficiently describe techniques for data extraction transformation and loading into a data warehouse</li>
    • Will possess an understanding of how to accurately and efficiently explain the techniques used for data presentation by analytical applications</li>
    • Will possess an understanding of how to accurately and efficiently demonstrate the techniques for building a dimensional data mart/warehouse.</li>
      </ul>
